The work and the artist.

SUPERSUN

SUPERSUN is the work of the artist Harry — a vintage illustration rooted in surf culture and the mystical power of the sun, and in the prism of light it casts across the world.

What began as a single drawing has become an iconic, globally recognized work — a quiet emblem for love and community, made personal one piece at a time.

Harry

Harry is a multidisciplinary artist recognized for provocative, narrative-driven work. A storyteller in every sense, he weaves often-dark themes through warmth, color, and a deep reverence for place.

SUPERSUN distills that instinct into its purest form: a single sun, a single word, made for the one person it's meant for.
